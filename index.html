Welcome to ChronoQuest 🎮

Master your time, track your focus, and gamify your productivity. ChronoQuest helps you stay on top of your day, one session at a time.

What you’ll find here:

  • Clock: Keep track of the current time with optional 24h or fuzzy display.
  • Alarms: Set up to 10 alarms, including voice commands.
  • Stopwatch: Measure elapsed time with lap functionality.
  • Timer / Focus Sessions: Set focus sessions and earn points when completed.
  • Daily Goals: Track how many focus sessions you complete each day.

Tip: Use voice commands (🎤) to add alarms quickly — works best in Chrome!
